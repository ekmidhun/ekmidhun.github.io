About Me

As a Ph.D. holder in Aerospace Engineering from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), I specialized in designing and implementing bearing-only measurement-based guidance for quadrotor UAVs in gap traversal scenarios. My research contributions include two articles published in the Journal of Guidance, Control, and Dynamics and five papers presented at prestigious international conferences. Additionally, I have over one and a half years of experience as an Assistant Professor at NIT Tiruchirappalli and as an Electronics Design Engineer at Greendzine Technologies. I am passionate about advancing aerospace engineering and contributing to the development of innovative and sustainable solutions for future challenges. My PhD was awarded on July 16, 2024. I then worked as a Research Associate at the Autonomous Vehicles Laboratory (AVL), Department of Aerospace Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, India. Currently, I am a Researcher at the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence group, Department of Computer Science, Electrical and Space Engineering, Lule√• University of Technology, Sweden, working on AI-based tree segmentation for virtual forest creation.
